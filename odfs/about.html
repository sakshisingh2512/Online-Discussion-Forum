About Us

Many education providers make up for the lack of social interaction between students by providing a student forum where the students can communicate and share their ideas or queries related to a particular topic. They might want to discuss a particular assignment that they are currently on or get answers to a question that they know they will receive a direct response for. So we are creating an open forum platform for students of a particular college where they can discuss there issues with their faculties along with the faculty of other department, alumni and colleagues within the college. So basically we are creating an environment in an individual college where all students can come in a single shed and can ask their queries or issues with the available person. Through this platform professors can also communicate quickly and efficiently with their students, since all the students are in one place.
